Postcard

Widespread Panic original · debuted Oct 6, 1986 · played 257 times
Original Writers Widespread Panic First played 10/6/86 Times played 257 ▶ Listen ♪ Lyrics 🎸 Tabs Every performance →

“When the band started we all lived in this little house in Athens, Georgia, called King Avenue, and we had a friend. His name was Tom Gunther. We commonly knew him as Bear. He left town… Some months later we got a postcard. It arrived at the band house. On the front side of it was a skier catching air way up in the mountains. On the back side was a post mark from Telluride, Colorado, and a very simple message: ‘The air here is great. We're having an ass-kicking time. This town is nuts. My kind of place. I don't ever want to leave.’”

Dave Schools · bass Colorado Music Hall of Fame induction · watch ↗

“…we had a buddy that wrote us a postcard and that turned into a song about 7 years ago. He gets little 15 cent royalty checks.”

John Bell · vocals KGSR, Austin · 4/12/97
